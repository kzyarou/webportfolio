About Me

Get to know more about me and my journey

I'm Zachary Paul Robillon Rapis, a Grade 12 student with over a year of experience in coding and web development. I specialize in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Java, and I've worked on multiple freelance projects that helped me develop real-world problem-solving skills. I'm also the developer behind STEMediaHub, a platform designed to bridge the gap between non-STEM or non-Grade 11 students and the STEM strand. As a dedicated full-stack developer, I'm passionate about building purposeful digital experiences. I believe in continuous growth, and I'm always ready to take on new challenges, learn new technologies, and turn ideas into impactful solutions.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, or sharing my knowledge with the developer community.
